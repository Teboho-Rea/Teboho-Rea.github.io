History

Our coffee shop was founded on a simple idea: to bring people together over a great cup of coffee. Our story began in 2023 when our founder, Ms Teboho Reabetswe Sepanya, had a vision to create a cozy and welcoming space where friends and strangers could gather to enjoy high-quality coffee and build community. Since then, we've grown into a beloved local institution, dedicated to serving the best coffee in town.

Philosophy

At The Cozy Cafe, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink - it's an experience. We're passionate about crafting each cup with care and attention to detail, using only the finest ingredients and sustainable practices. Our goal is to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where customers feel at home, and where every sip is a delight for the senses. We're committed to supporting local artists, musicians, and organizations, because we believe that coffee and community go hand-in-hand.

Commitment to Quality

We're dedicated to serving the highest-quality coffee, sourced from the finest small-batch roasters and expertly brewed to bring out the full flavor and aroma of each bean. Our baristas are trained to perfection, and we use only the best equipment and techniques to ensure every cup is consistently delicious. We're proud to offer a variety of blends and single-origin coffees, as well as delicious pastries and snacks to pair with your drink. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur or just looking for a great cup, we're committed to exceeding your expectations.

Meet Our Owner

Teboho Reabetswe Sepanya

Teboho is the proud owner of The Cozy Shop, and is passionate about creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for customers. With a background in culinary and being a barista, Teboho has always had a love for coffee and a desire to share that with others

Why she opened The Cozy Cafe

"I wanted to create a space where people could come together and feel at home. Coffee has a way of bringing people together, and

I wanted to share that with my community. I'm dedicated to serving the highest-quality coffee, and to making every customer feel

like family."
